# Axeo — Video Production Guide (No People)
## Two-Week Reel Series + Standalone Posts

---

## Platform Recommendations

### Tier 1 — Core Stack (use every post)

| Tool | Best for | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| **CapCut** | Stat cards, kinetic text, myth-busting formats, final Reel assembly | Free |
| **Canva** | Animated infographics, branded templates, story assets | Free / $15 mo |
| **Screen Studio** (Mac) | Cinematic app screen recordings with auto-zoom and device frames | $89 one-time |

### Tier 2 — AI Video Generation

| Tool | Best for | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| **Runway Gen-4** | Atmospheric B-roll, eye anatomy animation, abstract medical visuals | $12/mo (625 credits) |
| **Kling AI 1.6** | Product-style cinematic shots, abstract renders, smooth loops | Free tier (66 credits/day) |
| **Hailuo AI (MiniMax)** | Dramatic reveals, slow-motion abstract | Free tier available |

### Tier 3 — Pipeline (image → video)

| Tool | Best for | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| **Midjourney** | Generating source frames for eye anatomy, Ishihara plates, stat layouts | $10/mo |
| **Jitter.video** | Clean UI/product animations, floating app mockups | Free / $15 mo |
| **Rotato** | 3D iPhone/device mockups with animation for app demos | $9.99/mo |

### Recommended Workflow per Post Type

- **Screen recording posts** → Screen Studio → CapCut (captions + audio)
- **Stat card / kinetic text posts** → Canva or CapCut
- **Medical / anatomical posts** → Midjourney (frame) → Runway (animate)
- **Atmospheric B-roll posts** → Runway or Kling → CapCut (overlay text + audio)

## Audio Notes

All Reels — avoid copyrighted music. Use:
- **CapCut built-in library** (royalty-free, Reel-optimized)
- **Pixabay.com/music** (free, no attribution needed)
- **Uppbeat.io** (free tier, credit required in caption)

Recommended search terms per mood:
- Shock/stats posts → "countdown", "tension build", "revelation"
- Education posts → "clean", "minimal", "focus"
- Demo/app posts → "tech", "satisfying", "modern"
- Community/check-in → "warm", "uplifting", "gentle"
