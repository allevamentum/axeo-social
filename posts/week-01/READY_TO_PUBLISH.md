## HOW TO PUBLISH

1. Go to **business.facebook.com** → select **Axeo Vision**
2. Click **"Создать публикацию"** (Create Post)
3. Copy-paste the caption from above
4. Click **"Добавить фото"** → upload the screenshot
5. Either publish now OR click **"Завершить позже"** to schedule for a specific date/time
6. Both Facebook + Instagram will receive the post simultaneously
