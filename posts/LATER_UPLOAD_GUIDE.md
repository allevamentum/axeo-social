### How to Schedule Each Post in Later:
1. Drag an image from the media library onto a calendar time slot
2. In the post editor, paste the full caption from `later-week-1.json` or `later-week-2.json`
3. Select post type: **Reel**, **Carousel**, or **Feed Post**
4. Make sure **@axeo.vision** is selected as the profile
5. Click **"Schedule"**

### Format Guide:
- **Reel**: Best for engagement. Use for hooks and demos.
- **Carousel**: Use multiple images. First slide = hook, swipe for value.
- **Static**: Single image post. Use for facts, tips, stats.

### Pro Tips:
- Later auto-publishes to Instagram Business accounts
- You can bulk-schedule by dragging multiple images to different time slots
- Use Later's "Preview" tab to see how your grid will look
- Hashtag limit: 5 per post (Instagram 2026 rule)
